Chocolate Peanutbutter Pie

Description

This is the perfect low carb dessert recipe to finish up Thanksgiving. It's rich, creamy, and oh so chocolatey.

Ingredients

Chocolate Crust

  1. 1 cup almond flour
  2. 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  3. 1/4 cup powdered Sweetener
  4. 1/4 cup melted coconut oil
  5. 1 to 2 teaspoons water as needed

Peanut Butter Filling>

  1. 7 ounces dairy-free cream cheese, softened (Violife or Kite Hill)
  2. 1 cup coconut cream, room temperature
  3. 3/4 cup creamy peanut butter
  4. 1/2 cup powdered Sweetener
  5. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  6. 2 to 3 teaspoon coconut milk as needed
